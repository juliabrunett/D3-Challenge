Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Demographic Factors

The visualization above presents important links between health risks: (lacking healthcare, smoking, and obesity) and demographic factors: (age, household income, and poverty), by each state in the United States. This dataset, provided by the U.S. Census Bureau and the Center for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), is based on the 2014 American Community Survey 1-year estimate, along with the 2014 Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System Survey. The dataset can be explored by clicking each individual factor, which will show the correlation between one health risk and one demographic factor at a time.
